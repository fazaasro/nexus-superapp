## Skills Structure

Each skill follows OpenClaw's standard format:

```markdown
---
name: skill-name
version: 1.0.0
description: |
  What this skill does

when_to_use:
  - When to invoke this skill

when_not_to_use:
  - When NOT to invoke

tools_involved:
  - Tools used by skill

network_policy: restricted
allowed_domains:
  - example.com

expected_artifacts:
  - What the skill produces

success_criteria:
  - When work is "done"

---
## Workflows

### workflow_name
Steps and procedures

## Templates
Reusable templates

## Guardrails
Safety guidelines

## Negative Examples
Anti-patterns
```
